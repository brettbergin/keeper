About Keeper

Enterprise Secret Management

Keeper is a comprehensive enterprise secret management solution designed to securely store, manage, and synchronize sensitive information across multiple environments and services.

Key Features

  • Secure Storage: Advanced encryption and secure storage of secrets
  • Multi-Environment Sync: Synchronize secrets across AWS, HashiCorp Vault, and more
  • Role-Based Access: Granular permission control and user management
  • Audit Logging: Complete audit trail of all secret operations
  • Automatic Rotation: Scheduled and on-demand secret rotation
  • Approval Workflows: Multi-stage approval for sensitive operations

Supported Integrations

  • AWS Secrets Manager
  • HashiCorp Vault
  • Azure Key Vault
  • Google Secret Manager
